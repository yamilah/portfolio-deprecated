PROPOSAL

For my ScrollFlight site, I'm looking to curate a collection of prose I have written. It's a set of about ten poems. Currently, I have them laid out on my website with styled text, but it might be more interesting to curate them in a way that better reflects the narratives within them.

My goal is to critically examine the poems and select imagery that will best set the scene for them, and help to better deliver the content to the reader. I might have a large image prefacing each poem to act as a "palate cleanser," from one poem to the next. I might also have effects implemented between them that reflects each poem's tone (e.g., a poem with a sad tone might slowly fade in; a poignant and pithy poem might have a transition associated with it that is sharp and quick, etc.). I want to also be attentive to the color schemes I use, and this is something that can also change on scroll.

Over all, I'm looking for an interesting way to make it such that the reader will want to continue reading--not just for the content, but also to fully become immersed in the readings as multimedia experiences.
