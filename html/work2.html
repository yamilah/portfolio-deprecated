The Visual Design team at Intralinks was asked to create a vision for what a proposed feature may look like--the Admin Vision Dashboard. It's designed to help users receive and process meaningful data easily and quickly about their subscription with Intralinks' software. I was tasked with the visual direction of this feature, while still staying true to the Intralinks brand.
