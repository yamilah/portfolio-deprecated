Poetry Curation was a response to a need to show original poems in an interactive way. While physical books were always an option, I became interested in seeing how one could leverage traditional poetry with a modern form of interaction. For this, I used a library--ScrollFlight.js--to create the connection between the controls on the left and the transitions in the page itself. See the site live, here.
